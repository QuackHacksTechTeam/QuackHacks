    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is QuackHacks Lite?

    QuackHacks is an all day hackathon where high school students come together to build projects and learn new skills.

    When is QuackHacks Lite?

    QuackHacks Lite takes place May 31st, 9am - 6pm. Taking place in the ERB Memorial Union, in the Redwood Auditorium

    Who can participate?

    All High school students are welcome, regardless of experience level.

    Does it cost anything?

    Nope! QuackHacks is completely free to attend.
