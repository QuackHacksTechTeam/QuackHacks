Our Mission

QuackHacks is a fully student-run hackathon that empowers students to explore innovation through hands-on, collaborative learning. In a fast-paced and high-energy environment, participants work in teams to build creative projects, enjoy free food and merch, and gain exposure to new technologies through workshops and real-world experience.


As a registered non-profit, QuackHacks is organized by a diverse team of students from fields such as Art, Business, Marketing, and Computer Science. Our commitment to experiential learning extends beyond participants; organizers gain valuable real-world experience in areas like event planning, budgeting, sponsorship, branding, marketing, and software development. Every aspect of QuackHacks is designed to provide meaningful opportunities for students to apply their skills, grow professionally, and make an impact.
